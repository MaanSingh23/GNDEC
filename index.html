Our Campus

Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College offers a serene yet vibrant campus environment that blends academic excellence with rich cultural heritage. Spread across lush green grounds, the campus is home to state-of-the-art labs, spacious classrooms, and a well-stocked central library. Whether it’s the peaceful Gurudwara at the center or the energetic student life in hostels and sports grounds, GNDEC fosters both personal and professional growth. It’s not just a place to learn — it's a place to grow, innovate, and lead.

About
Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College

Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College (GNDEC), established in 1956 by Nankana Sahib Education Trust, is a premier institution in North India. It is an autonomous college affiliated with I.K. Gujral Punjab Technical University and is accredited by NAAC with an A Grade.

Known for academic excellence, research, and innovation, GNDEC offers AICTE-approved programs with ISO 9001:2008 certification, supporting holistic development in a vibrant and serene campus.

Admissions at GNDEC

Join one of North India's most prestigious engineering institutions.

1. Check Eligibility

10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Min 45% marks (general), 40% (reserved).

2. Apply Online

Fill the application form through our official admission portal or Punjab Technical University site.
